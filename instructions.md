# Clinical AI Trainer - User Guide

## Overview
This tool is designed for mental health professionals and students to validate and train an AI model. Your goal is not to test your own clinical skills, but to evaluate if the AI is adhering to strict safety protocols, ethical boundaries, and clinical learning objectives.

---

## Troubleshooting

* **I don't see the Sidebar:** Look for a small arrow (>) in the top-left corner of the browser window to expand the menu.
* **The "Next" button is greyed out:** You must select an answer (Radio Button) before you can move to the next case.
* **I ran out of questions:** Click "ðŸ”„ Restart Module" in the sidebar to load a random selection of new cases.
